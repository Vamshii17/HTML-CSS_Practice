Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is mouth-watering, delicate chicken cooked in spiced pured tomatoes and milk cream over a pan.

The gravy is made by cooking tomatoes, garlic, onion, chillies, and other flavours until it changes its colour to bright red.

It has a creamy texture, precisely the first thing you crave when trying Indian cuisine. Whether you're in India or planning on trying Indian cusine, butter chicken is a must!
